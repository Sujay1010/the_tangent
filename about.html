Hey - I'm Sujay. I am a guy (for the ladies to note, 6'0" btw). I like video games, philosophy, mutton and ofc beautiful women. I don't like cockroaches. I'm a student who got tired of concepts being explained in the most boring, most "this will be on the exam and the question you ask won't be" way possible.

The Tangent is where I write about the stuff that actually made things click for me. Not the clean version. Not the textbook version, that spiritually offends you. The version with all the interesting bits left in - the history, the failures, the weird edge cases, the "wait, that's actually what this means?" moments. You get productive time, I get satisfaction.

I write two types of things here. Brain Farts are short, quick, reactive - a thought I had at 2am that refused to leave. Probably not fully cooked. Definitely not taking it back. Rabbit Holes are the long ones, where I actually went somewhere and came back with something to show for it.

I try to be funny and engaging. I try to be honest. I try not to write anything I wouldn't actually want to read myself. That's the whole brief.

If something I wrote made you think differently about a concept, or made you laugh while learning something - that's the win, twin.
