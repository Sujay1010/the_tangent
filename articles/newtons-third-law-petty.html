Newton's third law is just the universe being petty
3 min read Physics February 2026

For every action there is an equal and opposite reaction. That's it. That's the law. The universe keeps score and it will not let anything go unanswered.

You push a wall. The wall pushes back. You're sitting in a chair right now and that chair is pushing up against you with exactly as much force as you're pushing down on it. The floor is pushing up on the chair. The Earth is pushing up on the floor. All the way down.

Nothing in the universe gets to exert a force without immediately having that force returned. No exceptions. No appeals. The universe is keeping receipts on every interaction and it will collect.

I think Newton's third law is the most morally coherent thing in physics. Not because consequences are good or fair. Just because they are inevitable. The universe doesn't do one-sided relationships.
